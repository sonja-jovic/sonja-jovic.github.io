HOW I WORK

I am currently taking a UI design course at uOttawa and learning about the principles of UI design. This portfolio will show my progress and designs created during the course. Although I'm still learning, my approach follows the visual communication principles learned in class and references such as NN/g. My design process starts by understanding the user needs and a product's context. Then, I move into sketching to produce a low fidelity prototype and receive feedback. Once I have a basic structure, I focus on applying things like color theory, typography, and spacing to ensure clear visual communication. Throughout this process, I always incorporate feedback from peers, instructors, and users, iterate on my design,and reflect on how well it can support usability and other user-centered aspects.
